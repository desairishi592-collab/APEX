FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is APEX free to use? +

Yes — APEX is currently in free open beta. Every feature is available with no scan limits while we're in beta.

Do I need to upload financial documents? +

No, it's optional. You can manually enter basic numbers, or upload a bank statement, CSV, or Excel file for a more detailed scan.

Can I analyze a public company instead of a private business? +

Yes. In investor mode, enter a ticker symbol to pull live stock data including price, P/E ratio, profit margin, ROE, beta, and more.

Is this financial advice? +

No. APEX provides data-driven analysis to inform your thinking, but you should always make the final call yourself or with a financial advisor you trust.

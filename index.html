APEX Agent

What it does

APEX Agent watches your watchlist and portfolio in the background — re-checking the APEX score, sector-calibrated sub-scores, and red flags for everything you track, and alerting you here when something meaningful changes. It also remembers your actual holdings when you chat, so questions like "how's my portfolio doing" or "what changed" get a real answer instead of a generic one.

Informational only — APEX Agent never places trades or takes action on your behalf.
